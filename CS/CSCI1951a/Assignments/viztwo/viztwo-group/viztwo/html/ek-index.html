Visualization 2

Donations by State

Description

For this visualization, I decided to show how much each of the United States (plus Washington DC) donated to BootLoader. Darker colors indicate larger donations. Beyond these more generalized price brackets, you can also hover over any state (or Washington DC) to see a tooltip with the specific value for the total donations that came from that state (or city).

Analysis

It seems to me that it would be in BootLoader's best interest to target users from Indiana and Georgia, as well as Oregon and Colorado, since users in those states seem to make the most donations. Regions with the lowest donations were the extreme Northeast (Maine, New Hampshire, and Vermont), in addition to other northern states like Montana, Wyoming, and North and South Dakota. These low values could be interpreted in multiple ways. For example, Bootloader could deploy extra targeting for these states to raise their donations, or do the opposite and ignore them since they've been historically low donators.
